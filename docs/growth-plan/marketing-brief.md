# Domani App - Marketing Brief for Landing Page

## Executive Summary

Domani is a mobile productivity app built on the psychology of evening planning. Users plan tomorrow's tasks at night when calm and reflective, rather than in the morning when rushed.

**Current Version**: 1.0.12
**Platforms**: iOS, Android
**Status**: Moving to Public Beta

---

## Core Value Proposition

### Tagline

**"Plan Tomorrow, Tonight"**

### Subheadline

"Make better decisions when you're calm, not rushed. Execute with clarity."

### The Problem We Solve

- Morning chaos and decision paralysis
- Task overwhelm from endless todo lists
- Anxiety from constantly re-planning
- Lack of daily focus and priorities

### Our Unique Approach

Unlike traditional todo apps, Domani shifts planning to the evening when users are calm, focused, and can think strategically. This addresses decision fatigue research showing better choices are made in reflective states vs. reactive states.

---

## Key Features

### 1. Evening Planning (Default 9 PM)

- Plan tomorrow's tasks during calm evening hours
- Add up to 3 tasks during trial, unlimited after purchase
- Assign categories: Work, Wellness, Personal, Education
- Set priority levels: High, Medium, Low

### 2. Most Important Task (MIT)

- Select exactly ONE task as your daily priority
- Prevents context-switching and scattered focus
- Prominently displayed on Today screen

### 3. Plan Locking

- Lock your plan to prevent midnight anxiety editing
- Commit to decisions made in your calm state
- Builds trust in your own planning

### 4. Today/Execution Screen (Default 8 AM reminder)

- Wake up to a pre-planned day
- Progress card shows completion percentage
- Focus card highlights MIT with "day theme"
- Check off tasks with satisfying animations

### 5. Smart Categories

- App learns your usage patterns
- Auto-sorts favorite categories for quick access
- Custom categories available after purchase

### 6. Customizable Reminders

- Evening planning reminder (customizable time)
- Morning execution reminder (optional)
- Timezone-aware scheduling
