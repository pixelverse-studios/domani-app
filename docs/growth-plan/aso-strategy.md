# Domani App Store Optimization (ASO) Strategy

> **App**: Domani - Evening Planning Productivity App
> **Platforms**: iOS App Store, Google Play Store
> **Status**: Public Beta

---

## Executive Summary

App Store Optimization (ASO) is the foundation of organic user acquisition, driving up to 70% of app installs. This document outlines Domani's comprehensive ASO strategy, including keyword optimization, visual assets, ratings management, and localization.

**Key Differentiator**: Domani is the only productivity app built around evening planning psychology, offering a one-time purchase model in a market saturated with subscriptions.

### Review Prompt Strategy

**Trigger Points** (High-satisfaction moments):

1. After completing all 3 daily tasks
2. After achieving a 7-day planning streak
3. After first week of consistent use
4. After completing MIT 5 times

**Prompt Timing**:

- Not on first day
- Not during trial expiration period
- Only after positive interaction
- Maximum once per 30 days

**Implementation**:

```typescript
// Trigger review prompt after positive moments
if (completedAllTasks && dayStreak >= 3 && !recentlyPrompted) {
  requestReview()
}
```

## App Store Featuring Strategy

### Apple App Store

**Nomination Process**:

1. Log into App Store Connect
2. Navigate to Featured > Nominations
3. Submit 6-8 weeks before target date

**What Apple Looks For**:

- Exceptional user experience
- Beautiful, intuitive design
- Latest Apple technologies (widgets, Siri Shortcuts)
- Universal app (iPhone + iPad)
- 4.2+ star rating
- Regular updates
- Localization

**Domani's Featuring Angles**:

- "Evening planning" unique psychology angle
- Clean, minimal design
- Focus on mental wellness/reduced anxiety
- One-time purchase model (user-friendly)

### Google Play

**Editorial Team Outreach**:

- Highlight unique positioning
- Emphasize Android-specific features
- Show strong retention metrics

**Collections to Target**:

- "Apps for productivity"
- "Self-improvement apps"
- "Apps to help you focus"
- "Hidden gems"
